# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 16, 2026

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal data and outlines our commitment to protecting your information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we manage your privacy.

---

## 1. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information when you use the Dog Translator App. You are not required to provide any personal details such as name, email, or address. We do not gather any data from your device, and there are no hidden data collection processes running in the background.

## 2. No User Tracking

The app does not track your location or any other personal activity. There are no features that monitor or record how you use the app. Your actions inside the app are not tracked or stored.

## 3. No Third-Party Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties. No data will be shared for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 4. No Advertising

We do not include any ads within the Dog Translator App. There are no third-party advertisements or marketing materials displayed to you while using the app.

## 5. No Analytics Tools

We do not use any analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to track user behavior or app usage. We do not collect data for statistical purposes, and there is no tracking of how often or in what ways the app is used.

## 6. No Data Storage

As we do not collect any personal or usage data, there is no data storage involved. Once you close the app, no information is retained or stored on your device or on our servers.

## 7. No Use of Your Data for Marketing

We do not use your information for any marketing or promotional activities. There will be no emails, messages, or notifications related to promotions, as we do not collect your contact information.

## 8. Privacy of Communications

The Dog Translator App does not allow communication features such as chats, messages, or social media sharing within the app. Therefore, there is no risk of sharing personal information through these features.

## 9. Device Permissions

The app does not request any unnecessary permissions on your device. We do not ask for access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location unless required for essential functions (such as the translation feature) and will only use those features with your consent.

## 10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or regulations. Any updates will be posted here with an updated effective date. We recommend you periodically review this page to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

---

*If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the App Store support channel.*
